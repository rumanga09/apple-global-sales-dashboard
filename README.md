Apple Global Revenue Performance Analysis (2022–2024)

Dataset from Kaggle: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/ashyou09/apple-global-product-sales-dataset

📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes global sales performance from 2022 to 2024 using Tableau Public. The objective is to evaluate revenue growth, identify key segment and category drivers, and assess regional contribution patterns through executive-style dashboards.

🎯 Objectives

Analyze Year-over-Year (YoY) and Quarter-over-Quarter (QoQ) revenue growth

Identify high-performing customer segments

Evaluate product category contribution to total revenue

Examine geographic revenue concentration

Simulate corporate-level performance reporting

📊 Dashboard Highlights

Revenue Trend Analysis (Quarterly & Yearly)

Segment Growth Comparison

Category Performance Evaluation

Geographic Revenue Distribution

Executive KPI Snapshot

🔍 Key Insights

2024 marks a strong recovery year following a 2023 contraction.

Government and Business segments drive revenue acceleration.

Mac category shows sustained upward performance.

Revenue remains concentrated in Europe and Asia.

🛠 Tools & Techniques

Tableau Public

Calculated Fields (YoY & QoQ Growth using LOOKUP)

Time-Series Analysis

Contribution & Mix Analysis

Corporate Dashboard Design Principles
